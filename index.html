Sudoku With Friends

To start a puzzle, paste a sudoku in .sdk format into the box underneath "Create New Room" then press the button. The room ID will be in the top right. Your friends can paste that into the "Join Room" box to collaborate on the puzzle with you. Need a puzzle? Try my New York Times Sudoku Importer chrome extension. Need a feature?
